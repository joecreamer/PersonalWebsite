Drake Maye's Statistics

He has a passer rating of 113.2 and has completed 71.9% of his passes, demonstrating strong performance since becoming the starter. On top of this he has turned his reciever Stefon Diggs into a WR1, who is on track for 1000+ recieving yards.

These stats highlight how effective Drake has been all season. His play his been phenomenal and his stats have never waivered from week to week. He is yet to throw under 200 yards in all of his wins, a remarkable level of consistency. Check out the full stats below.

His ability to stay calm under pressure has resulted in many close wins, but ultimately, the Patriots prevailed thanks to Drake. These are MVP-caliber stats for any quarterback in the league, and everyone should be surprised who the person that is doing it is.
